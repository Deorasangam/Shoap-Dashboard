# Shoap-Dashboard
-This dashboard provides an interactive overview of supermarket sales performance, focusing on sales, discounts, tax, customer type, and payment methods.

-This shows that discounts form a significant portion of pricing strategy, while tax impact is relatively small.
     Members generate significantly higher sales than normal customers

-Gross income and total sales are consistently higher for members
     E-wallet is the most preferred payment method
     Cash comes second
     Credit card usage is the lowest

- Digital payments dominate customer preference

Tools & Technologies

    Power BI – Dashboard creation & visualization

    Dataset – Supermarket sales transactional data

    Metrics Used – Sales, Discounts, Tax, Gross Income

- This dashboard helps businesses:

    Understand customer buying behavior
-Dashboard preview (https://github.com/Deorasangam/Shoap-Dashboard/blob/main/shoapsales.png)

    Identify profitable customer segments

Analyze payment trends

Optimize discount strategies
